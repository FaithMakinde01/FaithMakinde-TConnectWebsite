Connecting tomorrow's leaders with today's industries.

At Manchester Digital, we believe in unlocking the potential of young talent by connecting them to real-world opportunities. Our placement initiatives bridge the gap between education and industry, giving students hands-on experience, professional growth, and the confidence to thrive in their chosen fields. We’re not just helping young people find placements — we’re helping them build futures.

Why T Connect?

It’s structured, hands-on and designed around how the tech sector actually works. You’ll:

  • Rotate through different project types and employers
  • Work in agile teams, using real developer tools and workflows.
  • Gain exposure to a range of digital roles and workplaces
    • Gain access to additional tech skills training outside of your course delivery by people working in tech
  • Be part of a cohort (peer support group) with wraparound support and industry mentors.
  • Leave with contacts, experience and evidence that sets you apart

What will you be doing?

  1. Working from Manchester Digital’s central office with our friendly team.
  2. Working and contributing to real world project, both in teams and independently like building chat bots, websites or apps.
  3. Using tools like GitHub, Slack or Trello and joining in with agile ceremonies like stand-ups.
  4. Attending events and master classes led by tech professionals from acros the region.

What we expect from you!

  1. Be punctual: Arrive on time and let us know if something gets in the way.
  2. Be serious about your potential : We expect effort and engagement and good attitude.
  3. Get involved: Be curious about the tasks given and opportunities. Ask questions and sho initiative.
  4. Be open to feedback: We will help you to grow, but you need to listen and act on advice.
  5. Communicate: Keep in touch, share what is working and speak up when you need support.
  6. Work respectfully and collaborate with your mates.
